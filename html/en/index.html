Dream beyond dream

Internet has changed people's lives, and has created many Empires and heroes in new areas.

How many teenagers, in the road to pursue and realize dream, have made their dreams extended because of internet. As Zhong Zhaochuan, the father of the Internet said: If someone once said to their parents that he or she want to build a bigger shopping mall 10000 times than Beijing Wangfujing Department Store, if there was people once told his or her classmates that he or she want to single-handedly beat all auction houses in the world, he or she should have been sent to a mental hospital. But in the end, they not only realized their dreams, but dreams far beyond dreams they dreamed.

This is the beginning of story happening every day in the internet Ecology, even if the end is not wonderful, it can not stop the steps of dream.

As the Internet practitioners, every day we are thinking, we are committed to help people break all the limits, to make so many determined person find their dream home. However, confronted our own dream, what also can we do? Just all the way forward, to let our thought never stop, to let our steps to pursue dream with no limit!

TEDxTencent

TEDxTencent is a local non-profit activitiy organized for Tencent staff based on the official license approval from TED. So far, this is the first TEDx event organized in enterprise among BAT(Baidu, Alibaba, and Tencent) around the world. We are committed to bring Tencent employees Ted's way of thinking and speech, in the meantime to mine the internal unique and innovative ideas of Tencent, to encourage thinking creators use Ted's way of speech to spread their good ideas to Tencent. TEDxTencent is organized by SNG HR center and sponsored by Tencent Academy with team members and volunteers from TEG, S2, CDC and other partners who are keen on the spreading of TEDx.

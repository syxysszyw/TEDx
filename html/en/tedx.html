About TED and TEDx

TED (Technology, Entertainment and Design) is a private non-profit organization in the United States. Under the slogan "Ideas Worth Spreading", TED is well known for the TED conference. Each year in March, TED conference convened numerous outstanding figures in the field of science, design, literature, music in the United States, to share their thinking and exploration of technology, society and people.

TEDx program was created by TED in 2009 to encourage TED fans around the world to organize a TED-like event by themselves. As of April 2010, there have been more than 500 TEDx events organized in cities and villages around the world. The localized and self-organizing TEDx events can bring people gathered to share experiences similar to TED. In the TEDx, the combination of video and the speaker inspires a deeper discussion and closer connection among the group. This local, self-organizing activity is called TEDx, in which "X" represents “independently organized TED event”.
